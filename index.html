I build websites from scratch and focus on clean and functional design. I also revamp existing websites by updating the layout and content. My speciality is design customization, especially SVG logos for teams and organizations.

Bioinformatics

I have 15 years of experience as a bioinformatician. I take next-generation sequencing data and apply the latest tools in the field to answer important biological questions. I also generate pipelines that can transform raw DNA sequence to mutations of interest and reports that help inform clinicians on treatment decisions. My speciality areas are with parasite and cancer -omics.
