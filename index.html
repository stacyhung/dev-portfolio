What I do

Design + Development

I build websites from scratch and focus on clean and functional design. I also revamp existing websites by updating the layout and content. My speciality is design customization, especially SVG logos for teams and organizations.

Data Analytics

I work with lots and lots of data -- using this as input, I generate summaries and visualizations to provide a big picture of the data and understand knowledge gaps.

Bioinformatics

I have 15 years of experience as a bioinformatician. I take next-generation sequencing data and apply the latest tools in the field to answer important biological questions. I also generate pipelines that can transform raw DNA sequence to mutations of interest and reports that help inform clinicians on treatment decisions. My speciality areas are with parasite and cancer -omics.

Who I am

Designer & developer based out of Vancouver

I'm currently a Bioinformatics Scientist at the BC Cancer Research Center in beautiful Vancouver, Canada. In Dr. Christian Steidl's Lab, I have the unique opportunity of analyzing lymphoid cancer genomes and applying my computational expertise to distill meaning from all the A's, C's, G's and T's. I frequently collaborate with wet lab scientists and clinicians to gain biological insight and to brainstorm feasible workflows that can ultimately aid in therapeutic intervention.

Before coming to Vancouver, I was working on my PhD thesis, which explored the metabolisms of malaria and related parasites using bioinformatic approaches. I've always been a curious scientist, and my knack for programming has kept me in the bioinformatics field for over 15 years. I've also always had a passion for design, so I'm now pursuing front-end development in the hopes to be in a position where I can design beautiful, information-dense pages for a cutting-edge biotech company.

When I'm not on the computer, I'm travelling the world or on my road bike soaking up the mountain air and west coast sunshine.
